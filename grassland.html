Savannah Grassland Biome

Location

Savannah Grasslands are located in South America, Northern Australia, and about 50% of Africa (Central, East, and West).

Climate

Fauna (Animals)

In the Savannah Grasslands, the majority of animals consist of grazing mammals.

They include:

  • Zebras
  • Wildebeests
  • Elephants
  • Giraffes
  • Buffalos

Also, ostriches are found in Savannah Grasslands.

Flora (Plants)

The Savannah Grasslands also have unique flora. Just like the arid desert, Savannahs also have short trees like the acacia and baobab. Additionally, Candelabera trees grow there as well. There is also a large variety of grasses in the Savannahs, like Kangaroo Grass (Themeda), and Thatching Grass.

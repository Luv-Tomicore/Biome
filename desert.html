Arid Desert Biome

Location

The Arid Desert is one of the most commonly known biomes. They are located in Northern Africa, the Middle East, Central Australia, and Central Asia.

Climate

The Arid Desert is typically hot and dry year-round. The average temperature is 25°C to 50°C. Arid Deserts also get very little precipitation, which also leads to low humidity.

Fauna (Animals)

There are a variety of animals in the Arid Desert. Most of them are a large diversity of reptiles. There are also many marsipuals, like hares and sometimes camels. The animals have developed a habit of only coming out at night and staying inactive during the day.

Flora (Plants)

The Arid Desert is full of unique trees, shrubs and cacti. Typically, the trees found there include; acacia and baobab. The most common cacti found in the desert is the ball cactus. Additionally, the commonly found shrubs include; saltbushes and joint pines. The only flowering plant that survives in this desert is the daisy family.
